Here is how to run the PCB checker.
On the Desktop, double-click "launch_pcb_checker"
You will be prompted with a window, select the "Execute in terminal" button.

Now, the program is up and running!
For the first four seconds, there will be instructions on what buttons to press.
Then the Camera View will launch.
NOTE: If the camera view is too large and glitchy, double-click the top bar.
	- This will fit the window to view

CONTROLS:
Press "g" to take image of golden board.
Press "t" to take image of test board.
Press "a" to compare the two images.
Press "q" to exit.
